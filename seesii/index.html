Safety Notes Before You Cut

Always follow the user manual and use proper protective gear before operating any chainsaw.

  • Wear gloves and eye protection.
  • Keep loose clothing away from the chain.
  • Check chain tension before use.
  • Keep the chain properly lubricated.
  • Do not cut oversized or unstable materials.
  • Keep children and pets away from the work area.
