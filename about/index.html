About us

We care more about the right purchase than the quick click.

Our editorial standard

TestedHarbor is built around a simple promise: tell readers what we would buy with our own money, and explain the tradeoffs clearly enough that they can disagree intelligently.

We review product specs, owner complaints, pricing history, warranty terms, replacement parts, retailer availability, and hands-on impressions when possible. We avoid fake certainty. If a product is good but not for everyone, we say exactly who should skip it.

How affiliate links work

Some links may earn a commission. That helps fund the site, but it does not buy a recommendation. Products with high return rates, poor support, or misleading marketing are not worth promoting because they damage reader trust.

What we do not do

We do not publish paid placements disguised as reviews. We do not rank a product higher because it pays more. We also do not pretend every category needs an expensive upgrade pick.
